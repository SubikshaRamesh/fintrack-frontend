1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small data files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. Cookies are widely used by website owners to make their websites work, or to work more efficiently, as well as to provide reporting information.

Cookies set by the website owner (in this case, FinTrack) are called "first-party cookies". Cookies set by parties other than the website owner are called "third-party cookies". Third-party cookies enable third-party features or functionality to be provided on or through the website (such as advertising, interactive content, and analytics). The parties that set these third-party cookies can recognize your device both when it visits the website in question and also when it visits certain other websites.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

We use different types of cookies for different purposes:

Type of Cookie Purpose Duration
Essential Cookies These cookies are necessary for the website and application to function properly. They enable basic functions like page navigation, secure areas access, and account authentication. The website cannot function properly without these cookies. Session / Persistent
Functionality Cookies These cookies allow us to remember choices you make when you use our website and application, such as remembering your login details or language preference. They provide enhanced, more personal features. Persistent (1 year)
Performance Cookies These cookies collect information about how you use our website and application, which pages you visit, and if you experience any errors. They help us improve how our website works and understand which parts of the website are most popular. Persistent (90 days)
Analytics Cookies These cookies help us measure traffic patterns to determine which areas of our website and application have been visited. We use this to research user habits and improve our service based on user needs. Persistent (2 years)
Targeting/Advertising Cookies These cookies may be set through our site by our advertising partners. They may be used by those companies to build a profile of your interests and show you relevant advertisements on other sites. Persistent (1-2 years)

3. Third-Party Cookies

We allow certain third parties to place cookies on your device when you visit our website and use our application. These third parties include:

  • Analytics providers (such as Google Analytics) to help us understand how our website and application are being used
  • Advertising partners to provide personalized advertisements based on your interests
  • Social media platforms to enable features like sharing content
  • Payment processors for secure transaction handling
  • Customer support services to provide assistance when needed

These third parties may process your personal information according to their own privacy policies. We encourage you to read their privacy policies to understand how they process your information.

4. Managing Your Cookie Preferences

You can manage your cookie preferences in several ways:

  • Cookie Settings on Our Website: You can manage your cookie preferences by using our cookie banner or preference center.
  • Browser Settings: Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can usually find these settings in the "Options" or "Preferences" menu of your browser.
  • Device Settings: Mobile devices may have their own controls for cookies and tracking.
  • Opt-Out Programs: You can opt out of certain third-party cookies using tools like the Digital Advertising Alliance's WebChoices tool or the Network Advertising Initiative's opt-out page.

Please note that blocking some types of cookies may impact your experience on our website and the services we are able to offer. Essential cookies cannot be rejected as they are strictly necessary to provide you with the services you have requested.

5. Cookies in Mobile Applications

When you use our mobile application, we and our third-party partners may use similar technologies like mobile identifiers, SDKs (Software Development Kits), and device fingerprinting to identify your device and provide enhanced functionality. These technologies work similarly to cookies on websites but operate in the mobile app environment.

You can manage these preferences through your device settings or by adjusting the settings within our app. To opt out of mobile tracking on your device, you can review the instructions provided by your mobile device's operating system (iOS or Android).

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time in order to reflect changes to the cookies and related technologies we use or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. Please revisit this Cookie Policy regularly to stay informed about our use of cookies and related technologies.
